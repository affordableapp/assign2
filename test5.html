Sanmati cafe is situated in nitte,karkala which is famous for its delicious food.It has attracted many customers
and has become everyones favourite.There are variety of foods prepared by different chefs from across india.
Students go there when they are free becuase other than cafe there is a sanmati garden too where people can relax.
Students love the taste of food prepared there.And this cafe actually has good environment to eat because that matters the most
The taste and cost are very important for the people and its the only thing that attracts the people. In sanmati,
foods are affordable by all the people.

North indian dishes

There is almost all the north indian dishes like roti,parota,kofta,naan,butter naan,paneer butter masala, panner kulcha,veg kadai,fried rice and many other.

Icecreams

There are different types of icecreams like gadbad,dilkush,triple sundae,sanmati special icecream,canday, fruits salad etc.

Juices

There are different fruit juices like mango,watermelon,chikku,chakota.There are milkshakes too like chikku vailla milkshake, cherry banana milkshake and many other,Even we can get mojitos.
